<p><p>The ventilation masks market is categorized into two primary types: intrusive ventilation masks and non-invasive ventilation masks. Non-invasive ventilation masks, on the other hand, are designed to provide respiratory support without the need for intubation, typically used in conditions like sleep apnea or chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), enhancing patient comfort and ease of use.</p></p>
