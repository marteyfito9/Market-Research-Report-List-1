<p><p>The Cryosauna market offers three primary types of therapies: Liquid Nitrogen Therapy, Dry Ice Therapy, and Electric Cryotherapy. Liquid Nitrogen Therapy involves using nitrogen gas to chill the air in a chamber, effectively reducing skin temperature for therapeutic effects. Dry Ice Therapy utilizes solid carbon dioxide to create an intense cooling experience, aiding in recovery and pain relief. Electric Cryotherapy employs electric cooling systems to lower temperatures, providing a controlled and consistent environment for therapeutic benefits. Each method targets recovery, pain relief, and overall wellness.</p></p>
