<p><strong>The Automotive Fuel Delivery and Injection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Throttle Body Fuel Injector</li><li>Direct Fuel Injector</li><li>Sequential Fuel Injector</li><li>Port Fuel Injector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive fuel delivery and injection systems market includes various types of injectors that optimize engine performance and fuel efficiency. Throttle body fuel injectors mix fuel with air before entering the manifold, while direct fuel injectors spray fuel directly into the combustion chamber for better atomization and combustion efficiency. Sequential fuel injectors deliver fuel to each cylinder in a timed sequence, enhancing performance, and port fuel injectors spray fuel into intake ports, benefiting power delivery and emissions control.</p></p>
