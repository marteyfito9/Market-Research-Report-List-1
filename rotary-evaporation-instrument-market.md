<p><p>A rotary evaporation instrument, commonly known as a rotary evaporator, is a laboratory apparatus used to efficiently remove solvents from a sample through evaporation. It employs a rotating flask to create a thin film of the liquid, which increases the surface area for evaporation while reducing the boiling point. This method is widely used in chemical, pharmaceutical, and food industries for tasks such as solvent recovery and concentration of solutions.</p><p>The Rotary Evaporation Instrument Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by factors such as increased research and development activities across various sectors and rising demand for laboratory automation.
