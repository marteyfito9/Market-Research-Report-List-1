<p><p>The Indoor Environmental Monitoring market comprises various types, notably continuous and active monitoring systems. Continuous monitoring involves real-time assessment of indoor air quality parameters, such as temperature, humidity, and pollutant levels, providing ongoing data for prompt response. In contrast, active monitoring entails periodic assessments where devices are manually activated to collect data at specific intervals. This approach offers flexibility but may miss transient environmental changes. Both types aim to enhance indoor air quality and occupant comfort in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
