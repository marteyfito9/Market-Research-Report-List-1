<p><p>The spun silk yarn market includes several types, each catering to different needs. Ring spun yarn is produced through a twisting process, offering smoothness and strength, ideal for high-quality textiles. Open end (OE) yarn, made using rotor technology, is cost-effective and suitable for bulk applications. Polyester yarn blends durability and elasticity, expanding usage in versatile fabric applications. Semi-worsted yarn, made from a mix of worsted and woolen spinning processes, combines the softness of wool with strength, catering to refined garment production.</p></p>
