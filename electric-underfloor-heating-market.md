<p><strong>The Electric Underfloor Heating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heating Cable</li><li>Electrothermal</li><li>Carbon Crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric underfloor heating encompasses three main market types: heating cables, electrothermal systems, and carbon crystal solutions. Heating cables are installed directly under the flooring, providing efficient warmth through electrical resistance. Electrothermal systems utilize heating mats or films that uniformly distribute heat across the floor surface. Carbon crystal heating operates using thin carbon elements, offering rapid and even heating with low energy consumption. Each type caters to different installation preferences and energy efficiency needs, enhancing comfort in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
