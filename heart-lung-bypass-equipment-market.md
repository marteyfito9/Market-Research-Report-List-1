<p><p>The heart lung bypass equipment market is primarily segmented into two types: single roller pump and double roller pump cardiopulmonary bypass equipment. Single roller pump systems utilize a single pump to maintain blood flow during surgery, offering simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, double roller pump systems feature two pumps that enhance flow control and redundancy, improving safety and efficiency. Both types are essential for facilitating surgical procedures by artificially maintaining circulation and oxygenation of the blood.</p></p>
