<p><p>The cooking fat market is categorized into three main types: vegetable fat, animal fat, and mixed fat. Vegetable fat, derived from plant sources, offers healthier options like soybean or olive oil. Animal fat, sourced from livestock, includes butter and lard, providing rich flavors but often higher saturated fats. Mixed fat combines both vegetable and animal fats, aiming to balance health benefits and culinary qualities. Each type serves distinct consumer preferences, influencing their usage in various cuisines and health-conscious diets.</p></p>
