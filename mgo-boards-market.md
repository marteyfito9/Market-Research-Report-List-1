<p><ul><li>Thin (Thickness Below 8 mm)</li><li>Medium (Thickness 8-15 mm)</li><li>Thick (Thickness Above 15mm)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MgO boards market is categorized by thickness into three main types: thin, medium, and thick. Thin MgO boards, with a thickness below 8 mm, are lightweight and ideal for applications requiring flexibility, such as interior walls. Medium MgO boards, ranging from 8 to 15 mm, offer a balance of strength and insulation, suitable for both walls and flooring. Thick MgO boards, exceeding 15 mm, provide superior durability and fire resistance, making them perfect for structural applications and exteriors.</p></p>
