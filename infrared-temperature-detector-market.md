<p><p>The infrared temperature detector market is categorized into three types based on wavelength: Near Infrared (NIR) and Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR). NIR and SWIR detectors are used for applications requiring quick response times, often in industrial processes. MWIR sensors are effective for detecting heat in military and environmental monitoring. LWIR detectors excel in thermal imaging, commonly applied in medical diagnostics, building inspections, and security systems, benefiting from greater sensitivity to temperature variations.</p></p>
