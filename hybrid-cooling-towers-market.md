<p><ul><li>Direct Contact</li><li>Closed Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hybrid cooling towers market encompasses two primary types: direct contact and closed circuit systems. Direct contact cooling towers facilitate direct interaction between air and water, enhancing heat exchange efficiency, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, closed circuit cooling towers utilize a heat exchanger, allowing coolant to circulate without direct contact with ambient air, minimizing water evaporation and contamination. Both systems offer energy-efficient solutions, catering to diverse industries seeking optimized thermal management in various operational environments.</p></p>
