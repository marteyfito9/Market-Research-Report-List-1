<p><strong>The Adult Powdered Milk Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whole Milk Powder</li><li>Skim Milk Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adult powdered milk market encompasses two primary types: whole milk powder and skim milk powder. Whole milk powder is produced by evaporating the moisture from whole milk, retaining its fat and nutrients, making it rich in flavor and energy content. Skim milk powder, on the other hand, is made from milk with most of the fat removed, resulting in a lower-calorie option that retains essential proteins and nutrients. Both types serve various consumer needs in nutrition and food applications.</p></p>
