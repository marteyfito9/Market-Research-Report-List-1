<p><p>Tray seeders are essential tools in agriculture for efficient seed planting, with two primary market types: pneumatic needle and pneumatic drum seeders. Pneumatic needle seeders utilize a needle-like mechanism to plant seeds with precision, ensuring optimal spacing and reduced seed damage. In contrast, pneumatic drum seeders operate using a rotating drum to distribute seeds evenly across a larger area, making them ideal for bulk planting. Both types enhance planting efficiency and accuracy, catering to different agricultural needs and scales.</p></p>
