Active cooling pads utilize built-in fans or refrigeration mechanisms to lower the seat temperature, providing immediate and adjustable comfort. In contrast, passive cooling pads rely on materials that absorb and dissipate heat, offering a more gradual cooling effect without electronic components. Both types cater to consumer needs for comfort during hot weather, enhancing the driving experience through improved temperature management.</p></p>
