<p><ul><li>Pure Water-Jet Cutter</li><li>Abrasive Water-Jet Cutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5-axis water-jet cutter market is segmented into two main types: pure water-jet cutters and abrasive water-jet cutters. Pure water-jet cutters utilize high-pressure water streams to cut soft materials, such as food or thin plastics, without introducing any abrasives. Abrasive water-jet cutters, on the other hand, mix water with abrasive materials like garnet to enhance cutting efficiency, enabling them to sever harder substances, such as metals and stone. Both types offer precision and versatility across various industries.</p></p>
