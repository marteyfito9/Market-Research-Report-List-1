<p><p>The sterile filtration market comprises various membrane types, each suited for specific applications. MCE (Mixed Cellulose Ester) membrane filters are known for their high flow rates and compatibility with various solvents. Cellulose acetate membrane filters offer low protein binding, making them ideal for biological samples. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) membrane filters are chemically resistant, suitable for aggressive solvents and gases. Nylon membrane filters provide strong mechanical properties and hydrophilicity, suitable for both aqueous and organic solutions. Other filters include specialty membranes for niche applications.</p></p>
