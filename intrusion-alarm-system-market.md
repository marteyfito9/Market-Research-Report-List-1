Alarm Panels serve as the central hub, processing signals from detection devices and triggering alerts. Communication Devices facilitate immediate notification to users and emergency services, using technologies like GSM, Wi-Fi, or IP connectivity. Together, these components enhance security by providing timely alerts and effective monitoring of premises.</p></p>
