<p><p>The Metagenomic Sequencing Service Market encompasses various types that facilitate the analysis of complex microbial communities. Shotgun Metagenomic Sequencing allows for comprehensive genomic insights across diverse species. 16S rRNA Sequencing targets specific bacterial genes for community profiling. Metatranscriptomics focuses on analyzing RNA to understand gene expression within these communities. Whole-Genome Sequencing provides complete genetic information of individual organisms. De Novo Assembly reconstructs genomes from short sequences, facilitating the study of previously uncharacterized microbes, enhancing overall understanding of microbial diversity.</p></p>
