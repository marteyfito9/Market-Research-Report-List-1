<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Printing Inks</li><li>Water-based Printing Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screen inks market comprises primarily two types: solvent-based and water-based printing inks. Solvent-based inks utilize organic solvents for pigment dispersion, offering vibrant colors and excellent adhesion on various surfaces, ideal for outdoor applications. Conversely, water-based inks are formulated with water as the primary solvent, making them environmentally friendly and reducing volatile organic compound emissions. They are suitable for fabric and paper printing, providing a softer finish and ease of cleanup, thus appealing to eco-conscious consumers.</p></p>
