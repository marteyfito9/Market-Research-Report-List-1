<p><strong>The Foldable Smart Phones Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-folding Type</li><li>Out-folding Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foldable smartphone market is primarily categorized into two types: in-folding and out-folding. In-folding smartphones have screens that crease inward when closed, offering a compact design while protecting the display. Conversely, out-folding smartphones feature screens that fold outwards, exposing the display when folded, which can enhance usability but may risk screen damage. Both designs aim to blend portability with large display functionality, catering to consumer preferences for versatility and innovative technology in mobile devices.</p></p>
