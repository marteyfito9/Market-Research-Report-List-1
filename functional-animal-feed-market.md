<p><p>The functional animal feed market comprises two main types: functional feed additives and functional feed ingredients. Functional feed ingredients, on the other hand, are raw materials that offer health benefits beyond basic nutrition, such as prebiotics, omega-3 fatty acids, and natural antioxidants, promoting overall animal well-being and improving growth, reproduction, and immunity.</p></p>
