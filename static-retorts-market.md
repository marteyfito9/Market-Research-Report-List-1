<p><p>Static retorts are essential for food preservation, employing different heating methods to ensure product safety and quality. The steam and water spray retort combines steam and heated water for uniform temperature distribution, ideal for sensitive products. Water immersion retorts submerge containers in hot water, providing efficient heat transfer for diverse food items. Air steam retorts utilize hot air and steam, suitable for packaging that may not withstand water. Each type caters to specific processing requirements, enhancing food safety and shelf life.</p></p>
