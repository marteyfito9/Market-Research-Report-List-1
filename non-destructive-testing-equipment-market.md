<p><p>The Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) Equipment Market includes various techniques for evaluating material integrity without causing damage. X-Ray testing utilizes radiation to detect internal flaws, while Ultrasonic testing employs high-frequency sound waves for similar purposes. Magnetic particle testing identifies surface defects in ferromagnetic materials, and Penetrant flaw detection reveals surface cracks through dye application. Eddy current testing uses electromagnetic induction to detect surface and near-surface defects in conductive materials. Other methods may include thermal imaging and visual inspections, enhancing comprehensive material assessment.</p></p>
