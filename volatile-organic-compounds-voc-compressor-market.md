<p><p>The VOC compressor market includes two primary types: rotary compressors and scroll compressors. Rotary compressors utilize a rotating mechanism to compress air and are known for their efficiency and reliability in handling large volumes of gases. In contrast, scroll compressors use two spiral-like elements to compress gases, offering quieter operation and superior energy efficiency. Both types cater to different applications in industries that manage volatile organic compounds, focusing on environmental compliance and effective emission control.</p></p>
