<p><p>The large format printer market is primarily segmented into inkjet and laser printers. Inkjet printers utilize liquid ink sprayed through tiny nozzles, making them ideal for high-quality graphic prints and photos, suitable for signs and banners. They typically handle a wider color range and can print on various materials. Laser printers, on the other hand, use toner and a laser beam for fast, high-volume printing, excelling in text quality and efficiency, making them popular in architectural and engineering applications.</p></p>
