<p><strong>The Integrated Starter Generator (ISG) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Hybrid</li><li>Parallel Hybrid</li><li>Series Parallel Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Integrated Starter Generator (ISG) market encompasses three main types of hybrid systems: Series Hybrid, Parallel Hybrid, and Series-Parallel Hybrid. </p><p>In Series Hybrid systems, the ISG acts solely as a generator to provide electricity for an electric motor. In Parallel Hybrid systems, the ISG can function as both a generator and an electric motor, allowing for combined propulsion. Series-Parallel Hybrid systems blend both methods, enabling flexible energy management for improved efficiency and performance. These configurations enhance fuel economy and reduce emissions in vehicles.</p></p>
