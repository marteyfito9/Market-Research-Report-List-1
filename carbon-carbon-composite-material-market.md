<p><p>The carbon-carbon composite material market is categorized into three primary types based on their structural orientations. Unidirectional structure materials feature fibers aligned in a single direction, providing high strength along that axis. Bi-directional structure materials have fibers arranged in two perpendicular directions, enhancing strength and stability in multiple orientations. Multi-directional structure materials incorporate fibers in various directions, offering superior durability and resistance to complex loading conditions. Each type serves distinct applications, leveraging their unique mechanical properties for improved performance.</p></p>
