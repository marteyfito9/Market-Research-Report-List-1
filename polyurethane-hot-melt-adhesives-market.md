<p><ul><li>Non-Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives</li><li>Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane hot melt adhesives market is divided into two main types: non-reactive and reactive hot melt adhesives. Non-reactive hot melt adhesives are thermoplastic, providing immediate bonding upon cooling and are easy to apply, making them suitable for general applications like packaging and woodworking. Reactive hot melt adhesives, on the other hand, undergo a chemical curing process after application, resulting in stronger bonds and improved thermal stability, making them ideal for demanding applications in automotive and construction industries.</p></p>
