<p><strong>The Die Casting Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Pressure</li><li>Gravity</li><li>Low Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Die casting services encompass three primary market types: high pressure, gravity, and low pressure. High pressure die casting involves forcing molten metal into molds under high pressure for precise, complex parts, ideal for mass production. Gravity die casting relies on gravity to fill molds, producing larger, less intricate components with superior surface finish. Low pressure die casting uses controlled low pressure to push molten metal into molds, offering a balance between the precision of high pressure and the simplicity of gravity methods, suitable for various applications.</p></p>
