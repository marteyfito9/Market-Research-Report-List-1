<p><p>The Redox Flow Battery market encompasses two primary types: Vanadium Redox Flow Batteries (VRFB) and Hybrid Flow Batteries. VRFBs utilize vanadium ions in both electrolyte solutions, enhancing energy storage efficiency and longevity. They are ideal for grid-scale applications. Conversely, Hybrid Flow Batteries combine elements from traditional batteries and flow technologies, enabling improved energy density and faster response times. Both types cater to the growing demand for renewable energy integration and energy storage solutions, offering scalable and sustainable power options.</p></p>
