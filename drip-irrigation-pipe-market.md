<p><strong>The Drip Irrigation Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Drip Irrigation</li><li>Subsurface Drip Irrigation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drip irrigation pipe market is primarily categorized into two types: surface drip irrigation and subsurface drip irrigation. Surface drip irrigation involves laying pipes above the soil surface, delivering water directly to the plants, which enhances efficiency and reduces evaporation. In contrast, subsurface drip irrigation involves burying the pipes below the soil, allowing precise water delivery to plant roots while minimizing evaporation and runoff. Both systems optimize water use, contributing to sustainable agricultural practices and improved crop yields.</p></p>
