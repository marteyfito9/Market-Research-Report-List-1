<p><strong>The Horizontal Steam Sterilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity</li><li>SFPP</li><li>Pre-Vac</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal steam sterilizers are essential in healthcare for sterilizing instruments and materials. The Gravity type relies on gravity to displace air, making it effective for liquid loads. Steam Flush Pressure Pulse (SFPP) employs a combination of steam and pressure pulses to enhance sterilization, suitable for porous loads. Pre-Vacuum sterilizers utilize a vacuum to remove air before steam introduction, ensuring efficient penetration and effective sterilization of complex instruments. Each type serves distinct needs in maintaining sterile environments.</p></p>
