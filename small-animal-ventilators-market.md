<p><strong>The Small Animal Ventilators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Controlled Ventilator</li><li>Volume Controlled Ventilator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small animal ventilator market comprises two primary types: pressure-controlled ventilators and volume-controlled ventilators. Pressure-controlled ventilators deliver air based on a preset airway pressure, ensuring consistent respiratory support for animals with varying compliance. In contrast, volume-controlled ventilators provide a fixed volume of air with each breath, making them ideal for precise ventilation requirements. Both types are essential in veterinary medicine, supporting anesthesia and critical care by assisting or managing respiration in small animals effectively.</p></p>
